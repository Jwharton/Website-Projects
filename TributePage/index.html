Biography

Harrison Ford was born on July 13, 1942 in Chicago, Illinois. His father was an advertising executive and his mother was a radio actress. He has one brother. His hobbies as a child included Boy Scouts, in which he earned the second-highest rank, Life Scout. He majored in philosopy at the Ripon Colleg in Wisconsin. Before becoming a hit on the big screen he had serveral small tv appearances and even became a professional carpenter. His entry into the big leagues was through a role in American Graffitti in which George Lucus was the director. From there, George Lucas casted him in a starring role in Star Wars as Hans Solo. An avid air craft lover, he is both a helicopter and airplane pilot. His causes include Conservation International in which he is vice-chair, environmental documentaries, and being a spokeperson for EarthShare and Restore Hetch Hetchy, a valley apart of Yosemite National Park. He is currently married to Calista Flockhart and has 5 children.

Filmography

Harrison Ford is a world wide famous actor. His most well-known roles are Hans Solo from the original Star Wars movies, Indiana Jones from the Indiana Jones movies, and Jack Ryan in the popular action films, Patriot Games and Clear and Present Danger. His career has spanned over 60 years, with the total box office grosses of his movies well over 8.9 billion.
